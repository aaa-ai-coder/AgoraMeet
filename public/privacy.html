AgoraMeet Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Overview

AgoraMeet is a messaging app that lets you chat, make voice/video calls, and talk to an AI assistant. This policy explains what data we handle and why.

Information we collect

  • Phone number — used only to verify your identity via a Telegram confirmation. We do not sell or share it.
  • Profile — display name and phone, stored in your account to show friends/contacts.
  • Messages & calls — message text and call metadata (who you called, when) needed to deliver the service.
  • Media & AI — content you send to the AI assistant is processed via our AI provider to generate replies.
  • Device & crash data — anonymous error reports (app version, device model) used to fix bugs.

How we use data

  • Authenticate you and keep you signed in.
  • Deliver messages and connect calls (via Agora / Cloudflare).
  • Power the in-app AI assistant.
  • Improve reliability through crash reporting.

Third parties

We use Firebase (auth, database, hosting), Agora and Cloudflare (calls), an AI provider (assistant replies), and Telegram (login verification). Each handles data under their own policies.

Your choices

  • You can log out and request deletion of your account/data at any time.
  • You may use the app without the AI assistant.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the AgoraMeet team via the in-app support channel.
